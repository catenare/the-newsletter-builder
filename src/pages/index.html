Church

Following all our unexpected events, we finally had our church planning meeting for 2017. Our aim is to grow the church so that we are in the position to call a pastor full time. Our first endeavor towards growth will be the weekend of 19 February 2017, where we will do a door to door drive in handing out information regarding the church to the people in the neighborhood. We would like everyone in Klipspruit to know of Paseo Baptist Church.

With Chris and Hadessah having been such hard working and active members in the church, we are finding that there are new opportunities for others to step forward and take leadership positions. Due to the "big shoes" they left, it appears that the positions are intimidating to others, but we know that the Lord will raise up leaders that will fulfill the necessary positions. Our two biggest concerns are finding someone in the interim to do the preaching and a youth leader.

Family

Please continue to pray for Hadessah's family. Her husband Derrick, has been faithful in preaching weekly, however he has decided that he needs some time off from the preaching roster to help support his children and start the healing process in his own life. Hadessah left behind 4 children who are battling to find their way after their mothers' death. Please keep them in your prayers. As we have stated earlier, we might have tribulations, but we know that the Lord has overcome the world.

On a personal note, I'm still taking things one day at a time. The Lord is faithful in keeping me in good health and giving me strength for each new day. I've been so blessed to receive notification from two of my supporting churches that they will continue to support me for the 2017 year of which I can only give God all the honor and praise for.

Once again, thank you to all of you who continue to pray for the work here in South Africa and myself. It is through your prayers and financial contribution to the ministry that others can be reached for Christ.

Yours because of Calvary,
